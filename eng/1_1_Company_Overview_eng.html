ABOUT US

INTRO

SILLA Co., Ltd

Thank you for visiting SILLA Co.

Silla Co., Ltd was founded in 1967. It has engaged in trade (textiles exports) from the early business. Since 1972, The main business is fisheries (deep-sea fishery) until the present. Deep-sea fishery were started by the North Pacific trawler fishing and expanded as longline in 1988. And It is catching raw tuna for canning by purse seiner since 1990.

Also, our company was public offering of stocks in 1976 and won the best enterprise award from Korea Management Association. In addition, our company was awarded 'Gold Industrial Medal' and exported over $50 million. At this present, Silla Co., Ltd has invested domestic fishery, foreign fishery and seafood processing company. It is a mid-sized companies that holding 13 affiliated companies.
