SILLA's Steel Business

The steel distribution business, initiated in 2008 as part of business diversification, is actively supplying products tailored to demand based on trust.

Steel Business

'With over 15 years of expertise in supplying steel products, the staple of industries, promptly and accurately.'

SILLA's steel distribution provides a stable supply of various items tailored to customer needs, including carbon steel hot-rolled coils, galvanized steel sheets, wire rods, stainless steel wire rods, cold-rolled coils, plates, and structural shapes.

1. Leveraging experiences collaborating with excellent makers worldwide, such as China, India, Japan, Southeast Asia, and Europe, we are striving to become a leader in global steel distribution.
2. Building upon over 15 years of experience and infrastructure in the steel industry, we will continue to uphold the trust of numerous customers and makers.

DONGHWA FRESH PRODUCE

Agricultural Product Distribution

Established in 1977, Donghwa Fresh Produce is a distributor of agricultural product by auction at Garack market. The company was acquired by Silla in 2019, becoming a group company under Silla Group. Dongwha Fresh Produce seeks to develop agricultural distribution structure and to maintain appropriate price between supplier and consumers for stabilization of living.
